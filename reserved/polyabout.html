ABOUT

The goal of this website is to simplify the process of registration.

Some live far from registraton centers and some find it too involving to stand and wait in line. In consideration of all those people, this website was developed.

This site, not only simplifies the process but also, it is an efficient way as the data goes straight into the database of particular service provider.
